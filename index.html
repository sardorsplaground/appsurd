Frequently asked

Everything you need to know.

How quickly can I get started? expand_more
Once you subscribe, your dedicated project board is live within the hour. You can submit your first request immediately — most clients receive their first deliverable within 48 hours.
What kind of work can I request? expand_more
Anything in the software and design space: web apps, mobile apps (iOS/Android/Flutter), AI feature integration, backend APIs, DevOps/infrastructure, UI/UX design, branding, landing pages, dashboards, and more. If it's digital, we build it.
How does the "unlimited requests" model work? expand_more
You can add as many requests to your board as you want. We work through them in priority order — one (Standard) or two (Pro) at a time. There's no cap on the total number of requests or revisions.
What does "top 1% vetted" actually mean? expand_more
We accept less than 1% of engineers who apply. Our vetting process includes live technical interviews, architecture challenges, code review assessments, and a paid trial project. Every engineer must demonstrate both deep technical skill and fluency with AI-assisted development.
Do I own the code and designs? expand_more
Yes — 100%. Everything we build is yours. We transfer full ownership of all code, designs, and assets upon delivery. No licensing fees, no strings attached.
Can I pause or cancel anytime? expand_more
Absolutely. No annual contracts, no cancellation fees. Pause your subscription when you don't have active work (unused days roll over). Resume whenever you're ready. Cancel with one click.
